How do I create a work plan for the next weekly?

When you start to create your work plan for the next week, review your progress on your previous week's plan to help you modify your goals and create new tasks. If there were any tasks you didn't complete, consider placing them on your weekly work plan again or delegating them to someone else.

Why is weekly planning important?

Weekly planning gives you an understanding of certain aspects of your life like knowing priorities and sticking to them. And saying no is a big part of it. Weekly planning also helps you maintain balance in your schedule and in your life. All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. Life isn’t just about work. It’s a balance of both work and play.
